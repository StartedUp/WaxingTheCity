Cancellation Policy

We kindly ask that any cancellations or changes are made 24 hours prior to your appointment. A notice of less than 24 hours impacts our opportunity to serve other clients. Simply give the studio a call at 617-221-8999 when you need to modify, change, or cancel an appointment. Our studio coordinators are scheduling gurus! We understand “life happens” and you may miss an appointment without notice. However, if you miss more than one appointment , we reserve the right to charge a fee of up to 50% of the cost of the scheduled services for a late cancellation and 100% for a “No Show”. We require a Credit Card onfile for all appointments.
